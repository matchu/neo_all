Note: Collecting parts and creating Moltara Petpets is not part of the plot, it's an activity you can work on now that the city has been released.

Obsidian Quarry: Click on the Quarry (located on the bottom left corner of Moltara City) to collect another special item.



Miscellaneous Gears: Located randomly on the city map, you will find a pile of gears. Click on them to collect. You may have to refresh. (Look carefully!)

Scrap Metal and Stone: This can be obtained by a random event while refreshing on the Moltara City map.



Create New Petpets: After you have collected the Obsidian and Gears, take them to Tangor's Workshop and create a new petpet. (More petpets and combinations will be released as time goes on.)
